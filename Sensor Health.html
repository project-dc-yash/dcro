Hardware Connection Logic

All sensors are interfaced with the ESP32-WROOM-32. NIR and BME sensors utilize the I2C protocol (SDA: GPIO 21, SCL: GPIO 22), while the soil probe uses the ADC1_CH6 (GPIO 34) for analog readings.
